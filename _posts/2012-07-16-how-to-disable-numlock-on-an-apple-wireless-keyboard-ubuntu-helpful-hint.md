Just a quick tip for the day. If you purchase a new Apple Wireless Bluetooth Keyboard, and discover after pairing that it's only inputting numbers on the right side of the keyboard, it's because numlock is turned on.

<strong>To fix it, simply press fn+F6.</strong>
