It's rare that anything ever makes it to episode 250, let alone 1000, but Buzz Out Loud has done it!

<a href="http://bol.cnet.com" target="_blank">Buzz Out Loud</a> is the most amazing podcast, not to mention that it has the most amazing community surrounding it. Buzz Out Loud covers intersting and unique stories, mainly from the tech world, each week day. I must say, you haven't lived until you heard an official <a href="http://buzzoutloud.wikia.com/wiki/Mollyrant">Molly rant</a>, or the infamous <a href="http://twitter.com/acedtect" target="_blank">Tom Merritt</a> <a href="http://buzzoutloud.wikia.com/wiki/Pen_flip" target="_blank">pen flip.</a>

You can also spend time in the chat, where you'll find <a href="http://buzzoutloud.wikia.com/wiki/ChanBot" target="_blank">ChanBot</a> kicking people off for using caps, <a href="http://buzzoutloud.wikia.com/wiki/Gknee" target="_blank">Gknee</a> dropping admin knowledge, and the roar of dozens of chatters yelling "<a href="http://buzzoutloud.wikia.com/wiki/Mug" target="_blank">MUG</a>!" when one of the hosts proudly displays a CNET mug on camera (thank you <a href="http://buzzoutloud.wikia.com/wiki/Phatemokid" target="_blank">phatemokid</a>).

Just spreading the word. The 1000th episode is Thursday, June 18, at 1:30 PM Eastern (10:30 AM Pacific). It will feature many of the hosts from past episodes, favorite moments submitted from listeners, and even some videos (like<a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mucS7zsVWHc" target="_blank"> this one</a> submitted by <a href="http://buzzoutloud.wikia.com/wiki/Phatemokid" target="_blank">phatemokid</a> and <a href="http://twitter.com/nicnetter" target="_blank">nicnetter</a>). You can watch live at <a href="http://cnet.com/live/bol" target="_blank">http://www.cnet.com/live/bol.</a>
