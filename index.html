About Me

Hi, I'm Nathan. I'm an independent software developer and computer technician. You might know me as the guy making Vocal, a powerful podcast app for Linux that provides a one-of-a-kind user experience. I also created a small business to house my work called Needle & Thread. In addition to Vocal, Needle & Thread maintains a few open source projects (like the Exquisite Templates project), and offers technology services to people in the local community near my home in North Carolina. By day I am a computer technican and AP Computer Science Principles instructor. I'm also one of the hosts of a podcast about the elementary OS Linux distribution, called simply elementary.
